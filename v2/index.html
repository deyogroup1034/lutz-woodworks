About Lutz Woodworks
From high-rise corporate interiors to complex healthcare facilities and civic landmarks, we deliver the casework that buildings are remembered for.

Lutz Woodworks is a commercial subcontractor specializing in custom casework, millwork, and countertops. Since 1986, we've built our reputation on the things builders actually grade you on: hitting the schedule, manufacturing to precision, and standing behind our work.

As a Women-Owned business with four decades in the trade, we partner with owners, design professionals, and contractors who value accuracy and accountability — from budgeting and preconstruction through submittals, production, and installation.
